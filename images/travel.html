Château de Chenonceau, France
March 3rd, 2013

You might recognize the Castle of Chenonceau from tourist books, sightseeing websites, or from your fairy tale imagination. I loved seeing this castle and learning a bit more about it's contested history. If you're touring the Loire Valley -- this is a must see.
