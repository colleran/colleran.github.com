Château de Chenonceau, France

You might recognize the Castle of Chenonceau from tourist books, sightseeing websites, or from your fairy tale imagination. I loved seeing this castle and learning a bit more about it's contested history. If you're touring the Loire Valley — this is a must see.

Avignon, France
March 7th, 2013

The old town of Avignon is worth exploring and getting lost in. This part of the city is surrounded by fortified walls and maintains it's mideival feeling. Avignon was once the papal seat — supplanting the vatican for a few decades.

Montepellier, France
March 10th, 2013

Montpellier is a lively town that is dominated by it's student population. There is also a part of this city that is within a fortified wall, which makes it very walkable and resilient to modern development.
